About

Side-Scrolling Flyer is a web-based, Flappy Bird–inspired and Hugo Flying Level -game with more action, clever bots, and developer tools. Dodge obstacles, collect blue balls for fuel and score, and see how long you can survive— or let the built-in bot play for you!

Repo: https://github.com/pemmyz/js_hugoflights/

How to Play

Controls: Press Spacebar, ArrowUp, or Tap/Click the Screen to fly up.

Gamepad: Use the 'A' Button or Right Trigger to fly up.

Objective: Fly as far as you can and collect points!

  • Collect Blue Balls: Gain points and restore fuel.
  • Avoid Red Balls: These will damage your plane.
  • Avoid Thunder Clouds: These will severely damage your plane.

Your flight ends if you run out of Health or Fuel.

Press D-key to toggle developer mode.

Bot Controls (In-Game)

  • B Key: Toggle the autopilot Bot ON/OFF.
  • 1 Key: Activates "Collector Mode" (only seeks blue balls, ignores threats).
  • 2 Key: Activates "Smart Mode" (avoids threats & collects balls).
  • 3 Key: Activates "Avoider Mode" (cautiously avoids all threats, ignores collectibles).
  • 4 Key: Activates "Kamikaze Mode" (recklessly seeks out threats).
